About CAIL

Transforming academic interaction through verified institutional intelligence

Project Overview

CAIL (Campus Academic Intelligence Layer) is an institution-owned, curriculum-aligned academic intelligence system built exclusively on faculty-verified content.

Unlike generic AI assistants that pull information from the open internet, CAIL operates as a closed, verified academic ecosystem where every answer is derived from materials uploaded and approved by faculty members.

The Problem

Students face fragmented academic content, struggle to get quick answers to curriculum questions, and lose learning momentum when they miss classes. Faculty members spend excessive time answering repetitive queries.

Our Solution

CAIL centralizes all academic content in a verified knowledge base, provides instant AI-powered responses to student queries, and ensures learning continuity through automated summaries and guidance.
